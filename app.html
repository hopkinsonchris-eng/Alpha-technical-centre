HOW IT WORKS — Cost inputs apply across all wells. The Screener reads each well's IPR parameters, skin, lift method and current production rate from the Nodal Analysis and DHE tabs. For each applicable intervention type it calculates the incremental oil rate, revenue uplift, total cost (lump sum + rig/CT days × day rate), payout and 5-year NPV. Wells are ranked by NPV. The Portfolio Builder lets you select interventions within the capital budget and export Base + Optimised scenarios to the Financial Model.

INJECTION SETTINGS — Bo / Bw / Geo Factors
Formation volume factors convert surface volumes to reservoir conditions for VRR calculations. Geometrical factors allocate injection support from each injector to each producer over time.
FORMATION VOLUME FACTORS
Bo — Oil FVF (res bbl/STB)
Bw — Water FVF (res bbl/STB)
Bg — Gas FVF (res bbl/MSCF)
Binj — Injected water FVF (res bbl/STB)
PATTERN GEOMETRICAL FACTORS
Define patterns and allocate each injector's support across its producers. Factors for one injector should sum to 1.0. Add multiple rows for the same pair to model changing sweep over time.
