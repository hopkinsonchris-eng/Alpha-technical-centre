Ramp Construction · Best-First Sequencing on a Lognormal Well Stock

How the frozen production ramps were built; Lagunillas 2026–27 superseded by AICO actuals.

Reactivation rates across a mature brownfield well stock are strongly right-skewed: a minority of wells with preserved completions and higher remaining reservoir energy carry a disproportionate share of restorable rate. The engine represents each field's shut-in inventory as a lognormal distribution of expected restored rates, calibrated per field, then sequences reactivations in descending expected-rate order — best-first. The consequence is front-loading: the cheapest, highest-rate barrels land in Phase 1a, which is precisely the production that services and extinguishes the pre-financing facility, leaving Ph2/Ph3 funded from operating cash.
