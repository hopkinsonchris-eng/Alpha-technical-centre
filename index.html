What we do

From first screening to final investment decision

Three disciplines, one integrated workflow. Each feeds the next, so assumptions stay consistent from the rock all the way to the cash flow.

Oil & Gas Modelling

Petrophysics, static and dynamic reservoir models, production forecasting, and network hydraulics — grounded in the physics of the rock.

Financial Modelling

Transparent, auditable cash-flow models. Fiscal regimes, sensitivities and Monte Carlo — every number traceable to an assumption you can challenge.

For investors

Technical due diligence, before you write the cheque

Hedge funds and institutional investors bring us in on the buy side — to pressure-test the technical case behind an oil & gas deal, written by people who have actually developed and produced the assets.

Risk evaluation

Reserves, decline, cost and abandonment liabilities stress-tested against the physics — so the downside is known before it becomes a surprise.

Upside identification

The barrels the seller left off the page — behind-pipe zones, infill, artificial lift and recovery the data room has every reason not to show you.

Management & operator review

An independent read on whether the incumbent team can actually deliver the plan they are selling — capability, track record and execution risk.
