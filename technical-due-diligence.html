The mandate

Diligence written by people who have run the assets

Alpha Technical Centre sits on your side of the table. We are reservoir engineers and former operators — not analysts modelling from a data room. We pressure-test the subsurface, the production forecast and the people behind them, then hand you an independent technical opinion you can defend to your investment committee.

Risk evaluation

Independent reserves and technical risk assessment — decline behaviour, recovery assumptions, well integrity, and cost and abandonment exposure. We find the downside the pitch deck leaves out, and put a number on it.

Upside identification

Where is the value the current owner isn't pricing? Bypassed pay, infill and workover potential, artificial lift, recompletions and injection — the technical levers that move the return, quantified against the base case you're underwriting.

Management & operator review

A capital plan is only as good as the team executing it. We assess the incumbent management and operating team's technical track record, capability and credibility to deliver the case you're being asked to fund.

The edge

An operator's read, not a spreadsheet's

Buy-side technical diligence is only useful if the people writing it have actually developed and produced assets. Ours have.

A century in the field

A core team with a combined century of operating experience across Repsol, TOTAL, Shell, LUKOIL and Imperial Energy — decisions made with capital at risk, not just in a model.

Independent & conflict-free

We work for the investor, not the seller. No banking relationship, no fee riding on the deal closing — just the technical answer, fast enough to matter inside a live process.

Committee-ready deliverables

Competent-person-grade technical opinions, reserves and production commentary, and a clear risk-and-upside summary — written for investment committees and lenders, not just engineers.

Before you invest

Get the technical read before you write the cheque

Tell us the asset, the company or the operator and where you are in the process. We'll come back with scope, timeline and cost — and, when it counts, the independent technical opinion your decision rests on.
